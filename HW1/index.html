Theme

Nostalgia is what fueled the design of this web page.

I incorperated some nostalgic elements from my past because some of these things have shaped who I am today.

I wanted the color scheme to be playful while also emitting a dream-like atmosphere. Purples and pinks are often associated with these feelings, according to science.

About Me

My name is Maria Goman and I'm currently a student at the University of Montana majoring in Media Arts.

I've always been creative and artistic. Ever since I could remember, I was drawing, painting--whatever I could get my hands on. I'm taking this major to utilize my creative abilties.

I'm leaning towards the graphic design route as an outlet to portray my skills. I've already helped make logos for businesses of friends, family, and colleagues. It brings me a lot of joy that I feel like I'm helping other people with my skills.

I'm also pursuing a website certificate, because I've taken an interest in website design upon taking this degree. I'm not the greatest at it, because coding is a little challenging for me. I've never been the mathematical or linear thinking type but I'm still determined to improve my knowledge and understanding of website developement and design. My goal for this semester is to get to a point to where I can be comfortable making websites, and actually implement those skills to make websites for real-life people.

Fun Facts About Me

I'm a hobby baker and I run a small cottage bakery from my home. I listen to heavy metal music when I'm stressed and I also love to watch Korean dramas.
